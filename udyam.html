Udyam Registration (MSME)

Government Registration for Small & Medium Businesses

Why Register for Udyam?

expand_more

Udyam Registration provides official recognition as an MSME and offers several benefits:

  • Subsidy on Bank Loans (Interest rate concession)
  • Collateral-free loans under Government schemes
  • Access to Government Tenders & Procurements
  • Protection against delayed payments

New Udyam Registration

expand_more

For business owners who are registering for the first time.

Documents Required:

  • Aadhaar Card (Linked with Mobile Number)
  • PAN Card (Individual or Proprietor)
  • Bank Account Details (Account Number & IFSC Code)
  • Business Name & Address Proof
  • Nature of Business Activity (N.I.C Code selection provided at center)
