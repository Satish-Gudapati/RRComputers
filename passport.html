How It Works

  1. Visit Our Center / Send Documents

    Bring your original documents or share them via WhatsApp for verification.

  2. Application & Payment

    We fill the online form carefully and pay the government fees securely.

  3. Appointment Booking

    We book a convenient slot for you at the nearest Passport Seva Kendra (PSK).

  4. Visit PSK

    Visit the PSK on the appointment date for biometrics and document verification.

  5. Police Verification & Delivery

    After police verification, your passport will be dispatched by post.
